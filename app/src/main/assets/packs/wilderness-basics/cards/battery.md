## Field Use

- Download maps and save key information before leaving service.
- Carry backup power for the trip length and a delay.
- Use airplane mode when connectivity is not useful.
- Lower screen brightness and close nonessential apps.
- Keep devices dry and avoid exposing batteries to unnecessary cold or heat.
- Reserve a power margin for location checks, light, and contact attempts.

## Watchpoints

- Cold batteries can appear depleted even when capacity remains.
- Camera, video, GPS tracking, screen-on time, and poor signal searching can drain power quickly.
- A paper map, compass, and headlamp reduce dependence on a single device.
