## Field Use

- Identify likely water sources before travel and confirm whether they are seasonal.
- Carry enough water for the planned route plus a delay.
- Pack a treatment method, backup method, and container for untreated water.
- Keep clean water containers separate from collected untreated water.
- Refill before you are forced into a low-quality source.

## Watchpoints

- Heat, altitude, exertion, and wind can increase water needs.
- Muddy, stagnant, or downstream water may require extra settling or pre-filtering before treatment.
- If official local water guidance conflicts with this card, follow the local guidance.
