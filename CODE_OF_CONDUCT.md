# Code Of Conduct

POSA should be a practical, safety-conscious open-source project. Contributors are expected to be respectful, factual, and focused on improving the software and documentation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and specific.
- Respect different field experience levels.
- Ground safety claims in sources.
- Discuss risk clearly without alarmism.
- Keep public project spaces free of harassment, threats, and personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Knowingly adding unsafe or uncited survival/medical claims.
- Presenting unreviewed content as verified.
- Using the project to promote illegal activity or harm.

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, or block participants who undermine project safety or community trust.
