# Content Guidelines

POSA content should be practical, cited, licensed, and conservative.

## v0 Content Scope

Allowed:

- Water finding basics.
- Water storage basics.
- Fire preparation basics.
- Shelter basics.
- Navigation basics.
- Signaling basics.
- Battery conservation.
- Gear checklist.
- First-aid kit checklist.

Not allowed in v0:

- Medical treatment instructions.
- Drug dosage instructions.
- Dangerous improvised medical procedures.
- Unsupported survival claims.
- Copyrighted manuals copied without permission.
- Generated advice without source grounding.

## Source Requirements

Every content item needs:

- Source name.
- Source URL or publication reference.
- License.
- Pack name.
- Pack version.
- Review status.
- Last reviewed date.

If a source is not legally reusable, do not include its content in the app.

## Tone

Content should be direct, short, and field-readable. Avoid hype, fear-based language, and overconfidence.

## Medical Boundary

First-aid treatment guidance is excluded from v0. A first-aid kit checklist is acceptable because it is preparation-oriented, not treatment guidance.

Future medical or first-aid content requires:

- Licensed source material.
- Clear attribution.
- Review status.
- Safety disclaimer.
- Strict distinction between source excerpts and any generated synthesis.
